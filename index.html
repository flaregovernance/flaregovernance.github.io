Blockchain for Data

Flare is the blockchain for data — a decentralized network that gives developers trustless access to high-integrity data from other chains and the internet. By combining the Flare Time Series Oracle (FTSO), the State Connector, and F-Assets, Flare empowers builders to create cross-chain dapps that are more secure, scalable, and connected than ever before.

Our mission is to connect everything — every blockchain, every data source, every asset. Flare enables a new era of utility where smart contracts can trustlessly use external information, unlocking use cases that were previously impossible in decentralized systems.
